Disclaimer: The zones shown below represent **restricted parking areas** that require a valid permit at all times during their stated hours. A permit is still required during game days, events, or weekends unless otherwise stated. Always check posted signage.

Parking Zone “A”: Permit required daily, 5:00 p.m. – 8:00 a.m.
Parking Zone “B”: Permit required Monday - Friday, 7:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m. No permit required weekends and national holidays.
Parking Zone “C”: Permit required daily, 6:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.
Parking Zone “E”: Permit required at all times.
Hourly Pay Parking Lots: Paid parking available by the hour (rates and availability may vary).

Disclaimer: AMP Parking services provide real-time access to available paid parking spots across the WSU campus and along the College Hill border. For the best experience, you may need to enable location services on your device. Availability and pricing are subject to change, and AMP relies on user permissions to offer the most accurate results. For up-to-date information or to pay for parking, visit the AIMS Mobile Pay website.
