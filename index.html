Parking Information

Find all the details about parking on College Hill below:

  • Parking Zones: A, B, C, and D.
  • Permit Required: Yes, from 8 AM to 5 PM.
  • Visitor Parking: Available in Zone D.
  • Cost: $5/day for visitors.

Shuttle Information

The WSU Shuttle service provides convenient transportation around campus and College Hill. Key details:

  • Schedule: Every 15 minutes from 7 AM to 10 PM.
  • Routes: Main Campus, College Hill, and Downtown.
  • Cost: Free for students and staff with valid ID.
