Shuttle Information

Key details about shuttle services and parking for game days and events:

  • Game Day Parking: Affected parking lots close at 1 PM. Non-CAF permit holders must move their vehicles by 1 PM.
  • Park & Ride Shuttles: Free shuttles operate from 7 AM to 11:30 PM on game days. Move your vehicle to a Park & Ride lot after 1 PM.
  • Disability Parking: Disability spaces are unaffected during events. Permit holders may park in disability spaces until the end of their day.
  • Stadium Way Closure: Closed 3 hours prior to kickoff.

For more details, visit the Shuttle Information page.
