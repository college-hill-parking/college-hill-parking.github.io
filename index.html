Church Parking

Additional parking may be available at the following churches (donations requested):

  • Pullman Presbyterian Church
    1630 NE Stadium Way
    Price: $20 - $30 (varies by game)
  • Concordia Lutheran Church
    1015 NE Orchard Dr.
    Price: $20 - $40 (varies based on game attendance)
  • St. James Pullman Church
    1410 NE Stadium Way
    - $30 donation per game (cash only)
    - No vehicles larger than a pickup truck
    - No RVs or camping
    - First come, first serve
    - Lot closes when full, must vacate by 7 AM next day
  • Community Congregational United Church of Christ
    525 NE Campus Ave.
  • Sacred Heart Church
    400 NE Ash Street.
  • Trinity Lutheran Church
    1300 NE Lybecker Rd.
