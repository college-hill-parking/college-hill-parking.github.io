Disclaimer: The information provided below (including shuttle schedules and parking zone details) is based on previous years and is subject to change. For the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding football game day transit, please visit Pullman Transit's official Football Service page. Updated details are typically released shortly before the football game series begins.

General Information

  • All affected parking lots (see map) used for home football game event parking will close at 1pm.
  • Permit holders without a Cougar Athletic Fund (CAF) permit must move their vehicle by 1pm.
  • Disability Parking: Designated disability spaces remain available for permit holders.
  • Stadium Way Road closes 3 hours before kickoff.

Permit Holder Parking Options

  • FREE Park & Ride Shuttle available all day. Select lots operate from 7am.
  • All Park & Ride shuttles run until 11:30pm.
  • Permit holders can park in their lot until 1pm, then relocate to a FREE Park & Ride lot.

Park & Ride Lot Information

  • Lot 1 (Valley Road Playfields): Crimson and Gray Express buses every 8-10 minutes.
  • Lot 3 (Terre View & Hwy 270): Wheatland Express bus runs in the morning; Pullman Transit bus takes over after 4pm.

Paid Parking on College Hill

Additional parking may be available at the following churches (donations requested $20 - $40 on average):

  • Pullman Presbyterian Church
    1630 NE Stadium Way
    Price: $20 - $30 (varies by game)
  • Concordia Lutheran Church
    1015 NE Orchard Dr.
    Price: $20 - $40 (varies based on game attendance)
  • St. James Pullman Church
    1410 NE Stadium Way
    - $30 donation per game (cash only)
    - No vehicles larger than a pickup truck
    - No RVs or camping
    - First come, first serve
    - Lot closes when full, must vacate by 7 AM next day
  • Community Congregational United Church of Christ
    525 NE Campus Ave.
    - $20 parking
    - Cars only (no RVs or oversized vehicles)
    - Game day only
  • Sacred Heart Church
    400 NE Ash Street.
    - Donation $5–$10
    - Availability may vary depending on game
  • Trinity Lutheran Church
    1300 NE Lybecker Rd.
    - $20 per parking spot
    - Game day and limited overnight RV camping
    - 90–105 spots available
    - Multiple spots may be needed for larger vehicles
