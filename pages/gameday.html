General Information

  • All affected parking lots (see map) used for home football game event parking will close at 1pm.
  • Permit holders without a Cougar Athletic Fund (CAF) permit must move their vehicle by 1pm.
  • Disability Parking: Designated disability spaces remain available for permit holders.
  • Stadium Way Road closes 3 hours before kickoff.

Permit Holder Parking Options

  • FREE Park & Ride Shuttle available all day. Select lots operate from 7am.
  • All Park & Ride shuttles run until 11:30pm.
  • Permit holders can park in their lot until 1pm, then relocate to a FREE Park & Ride lot.

Park & Ride Lot Information

  • Lot 1 (Valley Road Playfields): Crimson and Gray Express buses every 8-10 minutes.
  • Lot 3 (Terre View & Hwy 270): Wheatland Express bus runs in the morning; Pullman Transit bus takes over after 4pm.
